# Refund Policy

**Yusr ERP System**

---

## 1. Overview

At Yusr, we aim to provide a reliable and high-quality service. This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which you may be entitled to a refund.

---

## 2. Free Trial

We offer a **14-day free trial** with no payment required. We encourage you to evaluate the Service thoroughly during this period. No refund is applicable to the free trial as no payment is collected.

---

## 3. Service Downtime Refund

We take service availability seriously. If the Yusr platform becomes **unavailable due to reasons within our control** — including outages, server failures, or critical bugs — for a period exceeding **24 consecutive hours**, you are entitled to a pro-rated refund for the days the service was inaccessible.

**How the refund is calculated:**

The refund amount is calculated based on the number of full days the service was unavailable, proportional to your subscription cost:

- **Monthly plan (SAR 125):** Refund = (number of unavailable days ÷ 30) × 125
- **Annual plan (SAR 1,500):** Refund = (number of unavailable days ÷ 365) × 1,500

**Example:** If you are on the monthly plan and the service is down for 3 full days, you are entitled to a refund of SAR 12.50.

---

## 4. What Does Not Qualify for a Refund

The following situations do not qualify for a refund:

- Planned maintenance communicated in advance (up to 4 hours per month)
- Downtime caused by factors outside our control (third-party infrastructure, internet providers, force majeure events)
- Service interruptions caused by the customer's own actions, misuse, or violation of the Terms of Service
- Partial unavailability of non-critical features
- Change of mind or unused subscription time (we do not offer prorated refunds for early cancellation)

---

## 5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at **yusrsystems@gmail.com** with the following information:

- Your account email address
- The dates and duration of the service downtime
- A brief description of the issue experienced

We will review your request and respond within **5 business days**. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within **10 business days**.

---

## 6. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. We do not offer refunds for the remaining time in a billing period upon voluntary cancellation.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated via email or within the platform.
