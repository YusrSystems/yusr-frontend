# Privacy Policy

**Yusr ERP System**

---

## 1. Introduction

Yusr ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when you use our ERP platform.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

**Account & Identity Data**

- Full name, email address, phone number
- Company name and commercial registration details
- Account credentials (passwords are stored in hashed form)

**Business Data**

- Financial records, invoices, and transactions entered into the system
- Inventory and product data
- Employee and customer records you add to the platform

**Usage & Technical Data**

- IP address and device information
- Browser type and operating system
- Pages visited and features used within the platform
- Log data and error reports

**Payment Data**

- Billing information and transaction records
- We do not store full payment card details; payment processing is handled by third-party providers.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- Provide and operate the Service
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Ensure ZATCA compliance features function correctly
- Send important service notifications and updates
- Respond to your support requests
- Improve and develop the platform
- Comply with applicable legal obligations in Saudi Arabia

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Location

Your data is stored on servers located in **Europe**. We apply appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data in transit and at rest, in accordance with applicable data protection standards.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

- **Service providers:** Hosting, payment processors, and infrastructure partners who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
- **Legal authorities:** Where required by Saudi Arabian law or a valid legal order.
- **ZATCA:** To the extent required for e-invoicing and tax compliance.

We do not share your business data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account termination:

- You may request an export of your business data within 30 days of termination.
- After 90 days from termination, your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
- **Portability:** Request your business data in a machine-readable format.
- **Objection:** Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **yusrsystems@gmail.com**.

---

## 8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no system is completely immune to security risks, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the platform (session management, authentication). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or within the platform. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.

---
